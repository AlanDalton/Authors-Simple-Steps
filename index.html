ℹ️ About

Five Simple Steps—accidental publishers who sold practical guides, pocket guides, & one-offs—closed in 2016. I’m trying to track the authors’ preferred ways of making their books available. I’m building on Rachel Andrew’s Future Simple Steps and Andy Croll’s The New Homes of Five Simple Steps Books.

If I need to update this list, please let me know on Twitter, Threads, Mastodon, or Bluesky, or through the Authors-Simple-Steps repository on GitHub.

You can check this webpage’s valid HTML, structured data, valid CSS, web accessibility evaluation, PageSpeed Insights (performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO), and carbon rating.
